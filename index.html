Co-op structure (5 mentions)
    Co-op (1 member 1 vote) structure! Co-op structure
    Getting new members it's essential for us to break even on rent +debt. We should have some sort of consensus (or near consensus) voting scheme for new members, which should include an interview by a committee of members. We should have flexible membership options, some of which don't involve renting specific space (ie a container) but just using the public space. Talking to David korman right now, he said he'd be down to pay $100 per month to be able to use the space to work on projects. He is also super interesting in doing new member interviews, which I highly recommend him for. He also suggested vetting new members for their skills related to good listening and non violent communication. Co-op structure Membership Expectations
